ABOUT

When a user searches a certain city, the webpage will be using a
free weather API and based on the data it retrieves, such as
temperature, humidity, etc, the website will put out music
that fits the feel of the weather in that city.
(e.g. if a city with rainy weather was searched then music related
to rain will be shown on the page to be played.)
